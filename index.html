Garden Grove is going through a global pandemic, a national rethinking of justice reform and a lack of local leadership.

I am running for City Council to be your local advocate, to bring your concerns to City Hall, and make sure our City’s budget reflects our City’s values and includes all of the community’s input.

BUDGET & PUBLIC SAFETY

Public Safety is much more than spending more on the Police Department. In fact, crime is lowered more by increasing funding to community support services. *
But in Garden Grove 51% of our General Fund goes to the police department budget alone.

As your Councilmember I will increase funding for community support service and funding must include greater input from the community.

COVID 19 RESPONSE

The government we depend on in emergencies has let us down during this pandemic.

This awful disease has cost lives and livelihoods. Businesses in Garden Grove are closing and desperately need help.

I have watched cars line-up for hours outside of food banks at the Orange County Labor Federation.

As your Councilmember I will fight for our cities fair share of relief funds from the state and federal government.

OPEN & RESPONSIVE GOVERNMENT

When I tried to reach my City Councilmember, it took time and many unanswered emails. I will be a councilmember who cares.

I will reach out to everyone and every business REGULARLY. I will seek your opinion, your voice on the matters that affect YOU.

THE DEMOCRATS CHOICE

I am a proud lifelong Democrat and I hope to earn your vote.
